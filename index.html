What You Mean To Me

I don't really know when it happened. Somewhere between our random late-night texts and your "good morning" messages, you quietly became the best part of my day — and I didn't even notice it happening until it already had.

On the days that feel heavy, when nothing seems to go right, your smile is honestly the one thing that makes it lighter. Even a tiny selfie or a voice note from you can flip my whole mood, and I don't think you even realize you're doing it.

Talking to you feels like peace. Not the boring kind — the kind where I can just be myself, say something stupid, and you'll either laugh at it or roast me for it, and somehow that's exactly what I need.

And the funniest part is, you make my life happier without even trying. No big gestures, no effort — just you, being you, texting me back, existing in my life. That's it. That's the whole secret.

A Letter For Honey

Hey Honey,

I'm not really good at writing things like this. I usually end up typing something, deleting it, typing it again, and then deleting that too because it doesn't sound "right". But I didn't want to delete this one. I want you to read exactly what I'm thinking, even if it's a little messy — just like everything else about me.

Maybe I don't always find the perfect words, but one thing I know for sure is that life feels better with you in it. Like, genuinely better. The kind of better I didn't even know I was missing until you showed up in it.

There are days I get busy, distracted, or just plain tired, and I'm sorry for those days. But even then, you're still the first person I want to talk to, and the last person on my mind before I fall asleep.

I made this whole website just for you. It took way longer than I'll ever admit out loud, and I rewrote half of it because nothing felt good enough — nothing ever feels good enough for you, honestly. But no matter how many websites exist on the internet, this one has only one purpose — to make you smile.

So if you're smiling right now, even just a tiny bit... then I did my job. 😊

— Darsh
